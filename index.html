Black Lives Matter!

For as long as we all can remember, the social movement for Black Lives Matter has been an essential and important part of our society. Currently, the black community still faces many issues from inequality to discrimination based on skin color and race. The goal is to advocate for them, fight for them, raise a voice and make a change!

This social movement plays a heavy role in our society. Everyday, there are more and more situations that occur which relates to inequality and discrimination based off of skin-color. The black community faces many hardships that are barely recognized. Many people come together as one to raise a voice and attempt to make a change.
